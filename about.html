ABOUT US

Suffolk’s fastest, finest independent letting agency
We specialise in fast-track, stress free lettings and property maintenance. We are an independent local company with over 10 years letting experience between us, enabling us to offer you 'best advice' at all times. We know firsthand how stressful finding the perfect property or tenant can be, which is why we are here to make things as simple, efficient and hassle free as possible.
We work in the busy expanding towns of Ipswich, Felixstowe, Woodbridge and Stowmarket. Our professionally qualified teams offer a dynamic approach to all of your property needs. We are members of the Deposit protection scheme (DPS), Office of fair trading (OFS), Financial Ombudsman Service (FOS) and the Letting Agents Association and adhere to their strict rules of conduct.
Our professionally qualified teams are on hand to offer an unrivalled personal service in a prompt and courteous manner. Let us find the perfect home or tenant for you today.
